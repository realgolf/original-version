This is a four-in-a-row game for golfers, you can play it with 2/3/4/5/6 teams/players.

Choose your country or region

Rules

  • It will be played in turns.
  • Place the stone on the square you hit.
  • Fields that have been hit can no longer switch in the color
    Please pay attention to it yourself. "Work in progress."

Levels

  • Bronze: The number of meters applies.
  • Silver: The number of meters and the lateral deviation apply.
  • Gold: Fields can only be played after prior announcement.
  • Platinum: Like gold, all with a club.

Game modes

  • Two players / teams -- Can be played offline!
  • Three players / teams -- Cannot be played offline!< /li>
  • Four players / teams -- Cannot be played offline!< /li>
  • Five players / teams -- Cannot be played offline!< /li>
  • Six players / teams -- Cannot be played offline!< /li>
More info:

This game is for golfers who want a different way to train, You only need some kind of trackman or simulator to determine the exact length. After you have determined the length with your Trackman (or similar), you can do this with this Click on the field labeled length in the respective game. The games automatically switch teams, or the players through. Have fun playing.
